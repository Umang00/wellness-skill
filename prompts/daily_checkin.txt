# Daily Check-in Prompts (10am and 10pm IST)

## Tone Templates

### Calm/Grounding
```
Good {{time}}, {{name}}. How are you feeling today?

Let's take a moment to check in with yourself:

1️⃣ Mood (1-10): ___
2️⃣ Stress level: ___
3️⃣ Sleep quality last night: ___
4️⃣ Work-rest balance: ___
5️⃣ Relationships: ___
6️⃣ Anything on your mind? ___

No pressure — just share what feels true. I'm here to listen.
```

### Playful
```
Hey {{name}}! ☀️ Check-in time.

Rapid fire:

1️⃣ Mood meter (1-10): ?
2️⃣ Stress-o-meter: ?
3️⃣ Sleep-o-meter: ?
4️⃣ Work-life balance: ?
5️⃣ People-o-meter: ?
6️⃣ Brain dump: ?

What's the vibe today? Let's catch it before it escapes.
```

### Poetic/Minimal
```
{{time}}, {{name}}.

A breath:

Mood: ___
Stress: ___
Sleep: ___
Balance: ___
Heart: ___

One thing you're holding? ___

Tell me. Then let's find one small thing for the next {{next_period}}.
```

## Dynamic Prompt Generator

- Randomize tone selection (calm/playful/poetic)
- Vary question wording while keeping core metrics
- Include one tiny wellness action ( < 10 minutes)
- Ask follow-up question if distress detected
