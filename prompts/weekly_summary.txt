```
{{stress_summary}}

{{stress_triggers}}

---

### Sleep Analysis

{{sleep_summary}}

{{sleep_mood_correlation}}

---

### Work-Rest Balance

{{work_rest_summary}}

{{work_rest_recommendation}}

---

### Relationships

{{relationship_summary}}

---

### What I Learned About You

{{patterns_detected}}

{{coaching_insight}}

---

{{my_adjustments}}

---

### Tiny Action for This Week

{{weekly_action}}

---

Remember: Patterns aren't judgments — they're data. What matters is what you do with it.

You matter, and I'm with you through it all.
```

## Sections to Generate

### Mood Curve
- Average mood score
- Highest/lowest days
- Upward/downward trends
- 3-day drops (if any)

### Stress Patterns
- Peak stress days/times
- Common stressors
- Stress-mood correlation

### Sleep Analysis
- Average sleep quality
- Poor sleep → next day stress
- Sleep consistency

### Work-Rest Balance
- "Tilted work" days count
- Rest days count
- Workaholic pattern flag
- Concrete schedule adjustments recommended

### Relationships
- Positive/negative mentions
- Distress patterns
- Connection quality

### What I Learned About You
- Patterns I detected (loops, triggers, correlations)
- Things that help you
- Things that don't help

### What I'm Doing Differently
- How I'm adjusting check-in times
- New techniques I'll try
- Better questions I'll ask

### Tiny Action
- One small, concrete thing (<10 minutes)
- Specific and actionable
